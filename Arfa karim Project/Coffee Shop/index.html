Our Story

Rooted in Craft,
Brewed with Soul

What began as a tiny corner roastery in Shoreditch in 2012 has grown into a beloved sanctuary — a place where coffee is not just a drink but a ritual worth slowing down for.

We source single-origin beans directly from farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia and Guatemala, then small-batch roast in-house to unlock the unique character of each harvest.

Mission

Serve ethically sourced coffee that honours every farmer behind the bean.

Vision

A world where every cup connects people to the stories behind the harvest.
