Our Philosophy

Born from Passion,
Roasted with Intention

Urban Brew started with a simple belief: that coffee is more than a morning routine. It's a catalyst for community, creativity, and calm.

We partner directly with family-owned farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra — ensuring fair wages and sustainable cultivation. Every bean is carefully evaluated, profiled, and roasted in-house in small batches to highlight its unique geographical terroir. Our baristas are sensory artists, committed to pouring perfection every single time.

Nabeel Malik Founder & Head Roaster
